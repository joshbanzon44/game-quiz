About Us

Our Goal and Purpose:

Here at GameQuiz, our goal is to help all gamers and soon-to-be gamers alike find a game suited for them. Through the specialized quiz we have created, we offer a simple method for individuals to discover a game that matches their interests. We are hopeful that what is offered to all through this website can be an assistive tool in the world of gaming. GameQuiz is grateful and appreciative of all visitors and users and wishes the best of regards to each and every gamers' endeavors.
