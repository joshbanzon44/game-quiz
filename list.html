List of Our Recommended Games

GameQuiz supports 15 varying games from 3 different genres. Each game is unique in its own way and can bring enjoyment to any and all gamers!

Genres:

  • Shooter

  • Shooter games are action video games where the focus is almost entirely on the defeat of the character's enemies using the weapons given to the player. They often test the player's spatial awareness, reflexes, and speed.

  • Jump to Shooter Genre

  • Survival

  • Survival games are games often set in hostile, intense, open-world environments. Players generally start with minimal equipment and are required to survive as long as possible by crafting tools, weapons, shelters, and collecting resources.

  • Jump to Surival Genre

  • Adventure

  • Adventure games are where the player assumes the role of a protagonist in an interactive story, driven by exploration and/or puzzle-solving. They have a strong focus on narrative, offering up an immersive and engaging story for the player to follow while also allowing character development through dialogue.

  • Jump to Adventure Genre

All Games:

Shooters

    • Valorant
    • Valorant is a team-based first-person tactical hero shooter set in the near future. Players play as one of a set of Agents, characters based on several countries and cultures around the world. It is a highly competitive 5v5 character-based tactical FPS where precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities.

    • Platforms: PC
    • Fortnite
    • Fortnite is distributed as three different game modes: Save the World, Battle Royale, and Creative. Save the World is a player-versus-environment cooperative game where you collect resources, save survivors, and defend equipment that helps to either collect data on the storm or to push back the storm. Battle Royale is a player-versus-player game for up to 100 players where they must scavenge for weapons, items, resources, and even vehicles while trying to stay alive be the last player standing. Creative is a sandbox game mode for players to freely experiment with the game's mechanics and elements.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, Nintendo Switch, Android
    • Escape from Tarkov
    • Escape from Tarkov is a multiplayer, realistic, and hardcore first-person shooter video game. Players are set in a militaristic simulation and join matches called "raids" in which they fight other players and bots for loot and aim to survive and escape. It is an extremely difficult game and not ideal for beginners, but rather experts looking for an intense experience.

    • Platforms: PC
    • Destiny 2
    • Destiny 2 is a first-person shooter game that incorporates role-playing and massively multiplayer online game (MMO) elements. It is set in a "mythic science fiction" world with a cinematic story, challenging co-op missions, and a variety of PvP modes. The world of Destiny 2 and the mysteries of the solar system are where you can experience responsive first-person shooter combat, unlock powerful elemental abilities, and collect unique gear to customize your "Guardian's" look and playstyle.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation
    • Superhot
    • SUPERHOT is a smash-hit virtual reality FPS where time moves only when you move. As the player, you attempt to take out enemy targets using guns and other weapons, while the time within the game progresses at normal speed only when the player moves; this creates the opportunity for you to assess the situation in slow motion and respond appropriately, making the gameplay similar to strategy video games. The game is presented in a minimalist art style, with enemies in red and weapons in black, in contrast to the otherwise white and grey environment.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, Nintendo Switch, Oculus Quest

Survival

    • Minecraft
    • In Minecraft, players explore a blocky, procedurally generated, three-dimensional world with virtually infinite terrain and may discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures, earthworks, and machines. This open-world, sandbox, survival game is well suited for beginners to video games and experts alike.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, Nintendo Switch, Android, iOS
    • Subnautica
    • Subnautica is a first-person, open-world survial game that takes place in the late 22nd century, when humanity begins to colonize planets in space. It's objective is to explore the ocean and survive its dangers, while completing tasks to advance the plot. Players can collect resources, construct tools, build bases, submersibles, and interact with the planet's wildlife.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, Nintendo Switch
    • Valheim
    • Valheim is an open-world survival game played from a third-person perspective and inspired by Norse mythology. As fallen Vikings, players must craft tools, build shelters and fight enemies to survive. The game uses distinctive low-resolution stylized 3D graphics, with a combat system inspired by action games. Players explore a world shrouded in mystery and can discover distinct environments with unique enemies to battle and secrets to uncover.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox
    • Terraria
    • Terraria is a 2D sandbox-survival game with gameplay that revolves around exploration, building, crafting, combat, survival, and mining, playable in both single-player and multiplayer modes. It focuses extensively on using components found through excavation and spelunking to create new items, structures, and machines. Terraria offers the ability to delve deep into cavernous expanses, test your mettle in combat, or even construct your own city!

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, Nintendo Switch, Nintendo 3DS, Android, iOS, Wii U
    • Raft
    • Raft is an open-world survival game that throws you and your friends into an epic oceanic adventure. Alone or together, players battle to survive a perilous voyage across a vast sea. Gather debris, scavenge reefs and build your own floating homes while unraveling the game's narrative.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation

Adventure

    • Hogwarts Legacy
    • Hogwarts Legacy is an immersive, open-world action-adventure RPG set in the world first introduced in the Harry Potter books. Your character is a student who holds the key to an ancient secret that threatens to tear the wizarding world apart. Embark on a journey through familiar and new locations as you explore and discover magical beasts, customize your character and craft potions, master spell casting, upgrade talents and become the wizard you want to be.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, Nintendo Switch
    • It Takes Two
    • It Takes Two is an innovative co-op adventure where uniquely varied gameplay and emotional storytelling intertwine in a fantastical journey. Play as the clashing couple Cody and May, two humans turned into dolls by a magic spell. Together, trapped in a fantastical world where the unpredictable hides around every corner, they are reluctantly challenged with saving their fractured relationship.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Playstation, Nintendo Switch
    • God of War
    • God of War is a third-person action-adventure video game that has received universal acclaim for its story, world design, art direction, music, graphics, combat system, and characters. It features an over-the-shoulder free camera and intense story with beautiful cinematics. The player controls the character Kratos and follows him and his son Atreus in combo-based combat and puzzle game elements. The game is loosely inspired by Norse mythology, with the majority of it set in ancient Scandinavia in the realm of Midgard.

    • Platforms: PC, Playstation
    • Roblox
    • Roblox is a platform that hosts more than 40 million user-generated experiences and lets users build their own from scratch. Within these experiences, users can play games, socialize with others, and earn and spend a virtual currency called Robux. It is a perfect game for young game enthusiasts and children who want to start playing video games. It offers endless creativity, imagination, and communication with other players to create amazing experiences.

    • Platforms: PC, Xbox, Android, iOS
    • Stray
    • Stray is a third-person adventure game where you play as a cat set amidst the detailed, neon-lit alleys of a decaying cybercity and the murky environments of its seedy underbelly. Roam surroundings high and low, defend against unforeseen threats and solve the mysteries of this unwelcoming place inhabited by curious droids and dangerous creatures.

    • Platforms: PC, Playstation
